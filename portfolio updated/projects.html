KJConnect

The KJConnect is an online portal for the students for all the departments in the college. The users can share notes, previous year question papers or any other study material through this app. The uploaded notes are received as a request by the admins and after approval, the notes get added to the main page and become publicly visible.

Highlights

  • Acts as a centralized point where the students can access the study materials shared by the other students.
  • Contains cards for each upload which directs the students to the link where the notes have been uploaded.
  • Features a notification panel where the students receive the necessary details regarding uploads and updates.
  • Access to a detailed map of the college facilitating them to locate any room in the college without any hassle.

Style & Share

"Style & Share" is an innovative web application that is redefining the way people interact with their wardrobes and connect within the fashion community. This unique platform offers an enticing blend of fashion, sustainability, and convenience, bringing together individuals seeking clothing materials and those looking to share their fashion treasures. With a visionary approach to fashion consumption, "Style & Share" introduces a fresh perspective on owning and experiencing clothing.

Highlights

  • Community-Driven Wardrobe Sharing: "Style & Share" provides a common ground for fashion enthusiasts to seamlessly share, buy, and rent clothing materials. It fosters a vibrant community where individuals can showcase their style, discover new trends, and engage with like-minded fashion aficionados.
  • Sustainable Fashion Experience:* Going beyond conventional fashion platforms, "Style & Share" encourages users to embrace a sustainable lifestyle by focusing on utilizing clothing materials for a specified duration. By renting clothing items on a daily basis, users can enjoy variety without the burden of ownership, contributing to reduced waste and a greener environment.
